This is entry number one. With the intensifying hopes that entry two will follow. This is my music space 😊, its a little plot of land on the internet somewhere by the digital mountains, where Ive been instructured to document everything. Who instructed me? Well the AI of course. I didn't like it first, now I'm warming up to the idea of having somewhere of my own even if no one ever visits it.

So what is IT? Well this is the first entry so I'm not entirely sure, but it feels like its going to be a way to hold my self accountable for staying passionate about my passion. Sounds easy, but hasn't felt easy enough in the past. I love music, I love building and every intersection between but when I don't contribute, consume or commit to any of it, I really start to doubt my passion, and it makes me feel bad. SO here I am.

Im a pretty busy person with work and class and the crushing pressure of trying to enjoy some form of youth but from today I stick to one project. Building music stuff! I might have dabbled a little before. Okay a lot. But everything is an elephant graveyard where every gravestone is a memory of failed passion. So I wont delve into it. Its a CLEAN slate.

Here I will be yapping as such, documenting my learning journey with audio processing and software, talking about the music world and learning more about the industry, studying all the cool tools that contribute to the audio world, and most excitingly, hopefully I talk a bit about some music: stuff I like, maybe stuff I dont and discovering new music.

That's it. Now all that matters, literally all the matters is making some form of start at some point, whether its tommorow, later tonight or even next week.

Wish me luck!
